# L2RR2L Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 2026

L2RR2L ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children who use our educational reading app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when children and their parents use L2RR2L.

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

L2RR2L is designed for children ages 4-6 and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We take children's privacy seriously and have implemented the following safeguards:

### Information We Collect

**From Parents:**
- Account email address (for login and account recovery)
- Password (stored securely using industry-standard encryption)

**From Children:**
- First name (for personalization)
- Age or date of birth (to customize difficulty levels)
- Avatar selection (for profile customization)
- Learning progress data (scores, completed lessons, time spent)

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- Location data
- Photos or videos
- Contact information from children
- Social media accounts
- Device identifiers for advertising purposes
- Any personal information beyond what is listed above

## How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

1. **Provide the service** - Enable children to play educational games and track their progress
2. **Personalize learning** - Adjust difficulty based on age and performance
3. **Show progress to parents** - Allow parents to view their child's learning achievements
4. **Improve our app** - Analyze aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to enhance the learning experience

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes
- Data is retained only as long as the account is active
- Parents may request deletion of their child's data at any time

## Parental Rights

Parents have the right to:

- Review their child's personal information
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of their child's account and data
- Refuse further collection of their child's information
- Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@l2rr2l.app

## Third-Party Services

L2RR2L uses the following third-party services:

**ElevenLabs (Text-to-Speech)**
- Used to provide voice pronunciation of words
- No personal information is shared; only the words to be spoken are sent
- Their privacy policy: https://elevenlabs.io/privacy

**Apple App Store**
- Handles app distribution and in-app purchases
- Subject to Apple's privacy policy

## No Advertising

L2RR2L does not display any advertisements to children. We do not use behavioral advertising or tracking.

## No Social Features

L2RR2L does not include:
- Chat or messaging features
- Social media integration
- User-generated content sharing
- Communication between users

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained while the account is active
- Upon account deletion, all personal data is removed within 30 days
- Anonymized, aggregated analytics may be retained for service improvement

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email to the registered parent account.
